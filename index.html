About Us

We believe pets should be treated fairly and should live a healthy, happy and safe life. Our range of products and services are created for that very purpose, we have 100% organic pet food, very safe and fun kennel services and beautiful grooming services designed to pamper your pooches. Hive Pet Care always will be a family owned and operated company.

 

Over time, since its beginnings in 2006, Hive Pet Care has evolved to be the healthy, safe and fun brand it is today.

 

Our Hive Pet Care team go above and beyond for customers, by being able to provide expert advice and good old fashioned customer service (including carrying your items to the car!)

 

But the thing that really separates us from all the other pet care stores in this world, is the fact that we truly care about your pets. We really do take such special time, effort and energy into making sure your pet is healthy, happy and safe. We don't do this for money, we do this because it is the right thing to do and keeps the world spinning smoother for everyone.

 

Each store is operated by animal specialists who are trained and knowledgeable across all areas of pet care. Our team, national and local, understand that pets are family and deserve the same level of comfort and care - we also believe we're responsible for making this a possibility.

 

That's why no matter what your budget is, you can find the best products for your pet available at Hive Pet Care!

 

Visit us in store or call us and find out for yourself!
